About Teenagers Department

Who We Are

The Teenagers Department is a vibrant family of young believers committed to growing in Christ, serving the church, and impacting our generation. We believe in nurturing talents, building strong foundations in the Word of God, and preparing teenagers to be effective leaders in the Kingdom.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To raise a generation of teenagers who are spiritually grounded, morally upright, and empowered to influence their world for Christ. We envision a community where every teenager discovers their purpose and walks boldly in faith.

Mission

To create an atmosphere of worship, service, and fellowship where teenagers can discover their gifts, build godly friendships, and grow into responsible leaders. We are committed to mentoring, equipping, and inspiring teens to make a lasting impact in their families, schools, and society.
